About Me

Hi! My name is Sarah Bouvier, and I am currently a fourth-year student at Northeastern University studying Business Administration with a concentration in Finance and a minor in Data Science. At Northeastern, I've had various meaningful academic, professional, and leadership experiences, that have helped shape my future path and promote professional and personal growth.

One of my best experiences while at Northeastern was participating in NUin Berlin during my freshman year, where I got to live and study abroad for my first semester. It was an incredible way to start my college journey and gave me a global perspective I carry with me today.

On campus, I am an active brother in Delta Sigma Pi, a co-ed professional business fraternity, where I have served in two leadership roles: VP of Scholarship and Awards and VP of Chapter Operations. I am also a member of Husky Ambassadors, where I work as both a tour guide and at the registration desk, representing Northeastern to prospective students and their families.

Professionally, I have had the opportunity to gain experience across multiple industries and types of roles. I spent several years working at a plant nursery in my hometown throughout high school and early college, starting as a cashier and eventually working as a back-end operations intern, focusing on inventory maintenance the summer after my freshman year of college. For my first co-op, I worked in Financial Planning and Analysis at Alnylam Pharmaceuticals, where I represented the Technical Operations and Quality teams and gained exposure to the world of biotech. Most recently, I completed my second co-op on the deal team at Boston Millennia Partners, a life sciences venture capital firm, where I gained hands-on experience evaluating investment opportunities in the life sciences space. I will be returning to the life sciences industry post grad, working in pharmaceutical market access consulting at IQVIA in their Seaport office!
