Hobbies and Personal Interests

Outside of school, work, and quality time with others, I really enjoy spending time across various hobbies, where I can develop new skills, express myself, or gain exposure to exciting new experiences.

One of my favorite things to do is travel. I had the amazing opportunity to study abroad in Berlin my freshman year, and while I was there I made the most of it by visiting 10 different countries across Europe! Getting to experience so many different cultures, languages, and places in such a short period of time was truly life changing and something I will always be grateful to have done. My favorite trip I did was either to Prague or Poland. Outside of Europe, I've loved visiting Nashville, Montreal, and Costa Rica!

Another hobby I really enjoy is thrifting. I love going to places like Goodwill and Savers to hunt for unique and affordable clothes and home decor. Beyond the fun of it, thrifting is something that I feel good about because it aligns with my values around sustainability and reducing waste. Giving items a second life instead of buying new is something I try to be intentional about.

Staying active is also a major part of my life. I spend many days at Marino Recreation Center, either working out or using the sauna. I love doing Pilates, going on walks, hiking, and running. Each of these bring me different types of enjoyment, whether it's a calming reset or a refreshing and uplifting workout. I have also recently been getting into weightlifting, which has been both challenging and rewarding.

Cooking is another passion of mine. I love trying new recipes, baking with my roommates (cookies, pies, brownies), and making food for and with my friends and family. There is something really special about sharing a meal you made from scratch. Some of my favorite things to make are banana pancakes, olive oil and broccoli pasta, and vodka sauce. My best friends and I also love making dip, especially guacamole, tzatziki, and spinach artichoke dip.

My Hobbies

  • Traveling and studying abroad
  • Thrifting and practicing sustainability
  • Pilates, walking, hiking, and running
  • Weightlifting (currently working on it!)
  • Cooking and baking for others
